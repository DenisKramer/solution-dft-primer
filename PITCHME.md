### Why atomistic modelling

All materials properties are defined by the position of the atoms. *In principle*, we can design by rearranging atoms and calculate resulting properties.

#### Benefits

- Cost
- Control
- (Scalability)

#### False beliefs

- Fast
- Easy
- Cheap
